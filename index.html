About the Series

The Summer Discovery Series is a weekend academic event organized by the Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies. It brings together scholars, seekers, and spiritual practitioners to explore themes at the intersection of science, consciousness, ethics, and cosmology. Our goal is to inspire deep reflection through dialogue that integrates knowledge and wisdom.
