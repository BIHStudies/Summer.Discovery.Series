About the Series

The Summer Discovery Series is a weekend academic event featuring presentations and discussions at the intersection of science, consciousness, ethics, and cosmology. Our goal is to create a space where scholars, seekers, and practitioners can explore deep questions with intellectual and spiritual rigor.
