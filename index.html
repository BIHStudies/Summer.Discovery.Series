About the Series

The Summer Discovery Series is a free, three-weekend online event exploring the ethical, philosophical, and metaphysical frontiers of science. Organized by the Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies (BIHS), the series brings together a distinguished group of 18 international scholars, 15 of whom hold doctoral degrees, to examine enduring questions at the intersection of consciousness, culture, and scientific inquiry.

Each weekend centers on one of four core themes. Through accessible presentations, panel discussions, and informal Zoom breakout rooms, participants will engage with deep ideas in a collegial and interactive setting.

The event is designed to encourage **dialogue, reflection, and community-building** among seekers, scholars, and practitioners alike. Each day includes time for informal breakout discussions, Q&A sessions, and generous breaks to allow for meaningful interaction.
