Welcome to the Canvas Guide

This guide will walk you through logging in to Canvas, creating accounts, and adding the Summer Discovery Series and Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam courses.

Using Canvas: Choose Your Path

The Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies (BIHS) currently offers two courses on Canvas: the Summer Discovery Series and the Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam. The instructions below will depend on your interest and prior enrollment.

Please review the options below before proceeding:

  • 1. Sign in to Canvas if you already have an account.
  • 2. Create a new account and add the Discovery Series.
  • 3. Create a new account and add the Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam.
  • 4. Add the Conceptual Study to your Discovery Series.
  • 5. Add the Discovery Series to your Conceptual Study.

Each option will guide you through the necessary steps.

1. Log in to Canvas

If you already have a Canvas account, simply log in with your email and password.

  • Enter your email and password.
  • Do not bypass the login using google or FB etc. It creates problems. Use email and password
  • Click "Log in" to access your dashboard.
  • Save your log in and password in a safe place.
