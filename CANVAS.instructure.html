Getting Started

This guide explains how to log in, create an account, and enroll in our BIHS courses.

Choose Your Path

We currently offer two Canvas courses. Use the steps below based on your situation:

  • 1. Sign in to BIHS Canvas
  • 2. New to Canvas? Add the Discovery Series
  • 3. New to Canvas? Add Srimad Bhagavatam
  • 4. Already in Bhagavatam? Add Discovery Series
  • 5. Already in Discovery Series? Add Bhagavatam

Login Tip

If your BIHS Canvas account was created using a username and password, type your username in the email field when logging in. Using your email might cause an error.

1. Sign in to BIHS CANVAS COURSES

If you already have a Canvas account, follow these steps. Note: even if the login form asks for your email, you may need to use your username instead.

  • Go to the Canvas login page.
  • Enter your email and password.
    ( Note: Try your to login with Username if fails with email.)
  • Click "Log in" to access your dashboard.
  • Save your login details in a secure place.


2. NEW TO BIHS CANVAS

Add The Summer Discovery Series

Click this link to access the BIHS CANVAS course: Summer Discovery Series Course Link

You will be asked to create a new account.

  • Enter your full name
  • Create a Username (please remember it!)
  • Create a password
  • Enter your email address
  • Use the code L6FWRW to add the Discovery Series to your account.
  • Save your Username and password in a safe place.

After submitting, you'll be redirected to a login screen.

Important: Even though the login screen asks for your “Email,” you need type the Username you just created. Entering your actual email address here often causes an error that says: “Email not found.” To avoid this, always use your Username—even if it asks for an email.



3. NEW TO BIHS CANVAS

Add A Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam

Click this link to access BIHS CANVAS course: A Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam

You will be asked to create a new account.

  • Create a username (please remember it!)
  • Create a password
  • Enter your email address
  • Use the code L6H6BG to add A Conseptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam to your account.
  • Save your log in and password in a safe place.

3. After submitting, you'll be redirected to a login screen.

Important: Even though it says “Email,” you must enter the Username you just created. This is a known issue that causes confusion.
