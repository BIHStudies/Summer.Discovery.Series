Using Canvas: Choose Your Path

The Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies (BIHS) currently offers two courses on Canvas: the Summer Discovery Series and the Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam. The instructions below will depend on your interest and prior enrollment.

Please review the options below before proceeding:

  • 1. Sign in to BIHS Canvas if you already have an account.
  • 2. NEW TO BIHS CANVAS: Add the Discovery Series.
  • 3. NEW TO BIHS CANVAS: Add the Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam.
  • 4. USING BIHS CANVAS for Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam and want to add the Discovery Series
  • 5. USING BIHS CANVAS for the Summer Discovery Series and want to add the Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam.

Each option will guide you through the necessary steps.

1. Sign in to BIHS Canvas

If you already have a Canvas account, follow these steps. Note: even if the login form asks for your email, you may need to use your username instead.

  • Go to the Canvas login page.
  • Enter your username and password. ( Note: Try your username if login fails with email.)
  • Click "Log in" to access your dashboard.
  • Save your login details in a secure place.

Direct Course Access Links :

  • Summer Discovery Series
  • Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam

If you are enrolled in both courses, either link will bring you to your Canvas dashboard, where you can switch between courses easily.
