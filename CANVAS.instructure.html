Canvas Instructions for BIHS Courses

Learn how to access and manage your BIHS courses on Canvas.

Welcome to the Canvas Guide

This guide will walk you through logging in to Canvas, creating accounts, and adding the Summer Discovery Series and Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam courses.

1. Log in to Canvas

If you already have a Canvas account, simply log in with your email and password.

  • Enter your email and password.
  • Click "Log in" to access your dashboard.
Log in to Canvas

2. Create a Free Student Account

If you're new to Canvas, you'll need to create a free student account.

  • Click the link below to sign up for a free account.
  • Complete your registration and you'll be able to access your BIHS courses.
Create Account

3. Add the Summer Discovery Series to Srimad Bhagavatam

If you're already enrolled in the Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam, you can add the Summer Discovery Series to your dashboard.

  • Log in to Canvas and go to your dashboard.
  • Use the link below to add the Summer Discovery Series to your course list.
Add Summer Discovery Series

4. Add the Conceptual Study of Srimad Bhagavatam to the Discovery Series

If you're enrolled in the Summer Discovery Series and want to add the Srimad Bhagavatam course, follow the steps below.

  • Log in to Canvas and open the Discovery Series course.
  • Click the link below to add Srimad Bhagavatam to your list of courses.
Add Srimad Bhagavatam
